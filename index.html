Hello, pioneers!

The WPU textbook exchange offers a cheaper and a hassle free way to buy and sell used textbooks on WPU's campus. It is an easy and a fast way to get those college textbooks. More importantly, you will no longer have to worry about credit card payments, shipping costs, and other outrageous costs that often come with textbooks thorough other services. WPU Textbook Exchange will allow you to buy and sell books directly with students on campus.
